Fry Bread

Mix Dry Ingredients

To make Fry Bread: First, heat cooking oil in a pot on medium heat. You will need the oil to be about 2 inches deep in the pan. While the oil is heating, mix 4 cups of all purpose flour, 3 tsp of baking powder, 1 tsp salt, and 1/4 cup white sugar. Mix the dry ingredients together.

Add Water to Make Dough

Add 1 3/4 cup luke warm water. Mix the dough together until the ingredients are incorporated. The dough will be sticky. Next pinch off pieces of dough that are about the size of an egg. Roll the dough in a ball and then use your fingers to work the dough into a disc that is thin in the middle and thick on the edges.

Fry The Dough

Gently lay the dough discs in the oil. **do not plop the dough in the oil or the hot oil could splash out and burn you** watch the fry bread in the oil. When one side is golden brown, use tongs to turn it over and cook the other side. Place cooked fry bread on a plate covered in paper towels. To keep warm, cover with a clean tea towel.
