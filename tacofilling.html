Taco Filling

Cook Ground Beef

To make taco filling: First brown 2 lbs of ground beef. When the ground beef is almost finished cooking, drain the grease and add one medium sized yellow onion chopped, two cans red kidney beans rinsed and drained, 2 cans petite diced tomatoes (drain one of the cans), one can of corn drained, 2 teaspoons ground cumin, 1 teaspoon onion powder, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, and salt and pepper to taste. Let the ground beef mixture simmer until heated through and onions are clear. Set taco filling aside while you prepare the toppings for Indian Tacos.

Prepare Toppings

  • 1 Head of Iceberg Lettuce Shredded

  • 1 lb grated cheese (cheddar or taco blend both work great)

  • 8 Roma tomateos washed and diced

  • Sour Cream

  • Salsa

Layer Filling and Toppings on Fry Bread

Put a fresh piece of Fry Bread on a plate and then beginn layering: First, the taco filling, next the shredded lettuce, some grated cheese, dice tomatoes, sour cream, and salsa. Now you have completed a delicious Indian Taco. Enjoy!
