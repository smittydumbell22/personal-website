Content

Indian Tacos Tutorial

This website will include two step-by-step tutorials. The tutorials will be accessed by link buttons. One tutorial will be how to make Indian Fry Bread. This will list all the ingredients and the steps to prepare the fry bread dough and then fry the fry bread. The second tutorial will have instructions on how to prepare the taco filling along with all the ingredients and how to layer the ingredients to complete the meal. The site will also include separate tabs for the ingredient lists. The last tab will be for ways you can eat Fry Bread as a dessert. The site will include images of what the result should look like as well as a photo of each of the ingredients.

Fry Bread and Indian Tacos

Fry Bread Tuorial

To make Fry Bread: First, heat cooking oil in a pot on medium heat. You will need the oil to be about 2 inches deep in the pan. While the oil is heating, mix 4 cups of all purpose flour, 3 tsp of baking powder, 1 tsp salt, and 1/4 cup white sugar. Mix the dry ingredients together then add 1 3/4 cup luke warm water. Mix the dough together until the ingredients are incorporated. The dough will be sticky. Next pinch off pieces of dough that are about the size of an egg. Roll the dough in a ball and then use your fingers to work the dough into a disc that is thin in the middle and thick on the edges. Gently lay the dough discs in the oil. **do not plop the dough in the oil or the hot oil could splash out and burn you** watch the fry bread in the oil. When one side is golden brown, use tongs to turn it over and cook the other side. Place cooked fry bread on a plate covered in paper towels. To keep warm, cover with a clean tea towel.

Taco Filling

To make taco filling: First brown 2 lbs of ground beef. When the ground beef is almost finished cooking, drain the grease and add one medium sized yellow onion chopped, two cans red kidney beans rinsed and drained, 2 cans petite diced tomatoes (drain one of the cans), one can of corn drained, 2 teaspoons ground cumin, 1 teaspoon onion powder, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, and salt and pepper to taste. Let the ground beef mixture simmer until heated through and onions are clear. Set taco filling aside while you prepare the toppings for Indian Tacos. You will need:

  • 1 Head of Iceberg Lettuce Shredded

  • 1 lb grated cheese (cheddar or taco blend both work great)

  • 8 Roma tomateos washed and diced

  • Sour Cream

  • Salsa
