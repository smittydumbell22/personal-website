Content

Indian Tacos Tutorial

This website will include two step-by-step tutorials. The tutorials will be accessed by link buttons. One tutorial will be how to make Indian Fry Bread. This will list all the ingredients and the steps to prepare the fry bread dough and then fry the fry bread. The second tutorial will have instructions on how to prepare the taco filling along with all the ingredients and how to layer the ingredients to complete the meal. The site will also include separate tabs for the ingredient lists. The last tab will be for ways you can eat Fry Bread as a dessert. The site will include images of what the result should look like as well as a photo of each of the ingredients.

Fry Bread and Indian Tacos

Fry Bread Tuorial

Here readers will have access to step-by-step instructions for making fry bread dough and then forming it into the pieces and frying the bread.

Ingredients for Fry Bread

Taco Filling

Here readers will have access to the list of ingredients and step-by-step instructions to make the taco filling. The steps will include how to brown the ground beef on a medium temperature. Then add the chopped yellow onion, drained canned corn, cumin, salt and pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, and the drained diced tomatoes. The instructions will instruct the readers to let ingredients simmer until everything is heated through.
